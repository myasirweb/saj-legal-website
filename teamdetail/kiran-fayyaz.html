Biography

Dynamic and experienced in administrative functions, Kiran is an asset to S. A. J Legal and handles the firm's management and operations. She was born and educated in Lahore, Pakistan, and obtained her Bachelors in Commerce from the prestigious University of Central Punjab. Upon completion of her undergraduate degree, she was recruited by a renowned private school to undertake the administrative running of its day-to-day activities, supervise and improve operational effectiveness as well as liaise with various departments.

Driven by her ambitions, Kiran emigrated to the United Kingdom to undertake Masters in Business Administration from the Anglia Ruskin University. However, as luck would have it, her growing interest in the field of immigration, prompted by her own experience, convinced her to pursue a career in legal sector. She has since then been associated and actively involved in assisting with case preparations, management and client dealing. In order to further her professional development, Kiran is currently taking the Solicitors Qualifying Examination to become a solicitor.

Biography

Yasir manages the costs portfolio at S. A. J Legal. He has 5 years of work experience as a skilled and accomplished cost analyst and draftsman. Yasir has worked as a Costs Draftsman at Law Lane Solicitors and settled more than 150 cases in 5 years for Law Lane Solicitors alone.

He also worked as a Free Lance costs Draftsman with Ashton Ross Law, Imperium Chambers, Lincolns Solicitors, Lexpert Solicitors LLP and Sharif Mckenzie Solicitors. Yasir's caseload revolves around all types of immigration work as well as drafting bill of costs in immigration, personal injury, clinical negligence, housing and other matters.

Practice Area

Yasir possesses exceptional analytical, budgeting, negotiation and drafting skills. He is well versed with drafting the bill of costs, preparing schedule of costs and statement of costs. He is able to dissect and prepare points-of-dispute and points-in-reply, costs budget, provisional and detailed assessments, final costs certificate, default costs certificate and set aside default costs certificate applications.

Yasir's problem-solving skills and meticulous attention to detail make him a very competent and dependable team member whose work is central to the effective functioning of the other departments at the firm.

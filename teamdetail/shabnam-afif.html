Biography

Shabnam Afif is an Associate and Fee Earner at S.A.J Legal. She holds a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) degree from the University of Canterbury, Kent, and is currently pursuing both a Master of Laws (LLM) and the Solicitors Qualifying Examination (SQE).

Shabnam specialises in immigration law, with expertise spanning asylum, appeals, applications, business immigration, and other related immigration matters. She is dedicated to delivering professional, meticulous, and compassionate legal support, ensuring her clients are guided through complex processes with clarity and care.

Her commitment to excellence and client-focused approach reflects the high standards upheld at S.A.J Legal.

Public Law & Judicial Review

Hold Public Authorities Accountable with Our Expert Public Law & Judicial Review Solicitors

Public law governs the relationship between individuals and the state. It addresses how public power is exercised, the legal obligations of public bodies, and the mechanisms by which those bodies are held accountable. Our public law solicitors help clients understand, assess, and challenge the decisions of public authorities. A central mechanism in this area is judicial review, which allows courts to examine the legality of decisions made by public bodies. In the UK, judicial review UK acts as a vital safeguard to ensure that authorities remain within the scope of their legal powers and uphold their statutory duties.

Our Expertise

Championing Public Interest Litigation and Judicial Review

Civil Litigation & Dispute Resolution

We advise on a wide range of legal matters daily. It is essential to understand the available options and the associated costs. Our experienced civil litigation lawyers and dispute resolution solicitors provide tailored guidance on all types of disputes, from resolving family disagreements and trustee claims to property and commercial disputes involving significant sums. Breach of Contract Claims

If you are dealing with a breach of contract claim, our civil litigation lawyers will guide you through the process of securing compensation for your losses. We advise on a broad range of disputes, including those between business partners, directors, and shareholders, as well as matters involving employment, commercial, consumer, and construction contracts, tenancy agreements, leases, and international agreements. Our dispute resolution solicitors aim to restore your position by interpreting contract terms clearly and resolving matters through litigation or alternative dispute resolution when suitable.

Professional Discipline

We represent professionals including lawyers, doctors, healthcare providers, financial advisors, and accountants facing disciplinary action from their regulatory bodies. Our civil litigation lawyers understand how such proceedings can affect your career, reputation, and well-being. We provide support during investigations, strong representation at hearings or tribunals, and strategic advice on managing reputational risk, helping you navigate these challenges with confidence.

Professional Negligence

When a professional’s actions fall below expected standards and result in financial loss, a negligence claim may be appropriate. Our dispute resolution solicitors represent both professionals defending claims and clients pursuing compensation. We offer realistic, straightforward advice, pursue fair settlements, and, when needed, provide skilled courtroom representation. Our focus is always on resolving matters effectively and efficiently.

Comprehensive Family Law Services

We offer a comprehensive range of family law services to clients in London, the UK, and internationally. Our services include.

Divorce and Separation

We understand that divorce and separation can be emotionally and financially difficult. As experienced divorce and separation solicitors, we guide clients through every stage of the process. Our team helps you make informed decisions about property, finances, and parenting arrangements, always with your long-term interests in mind. When litigation is necessary, we provide robust representation and practical divorce and separation advice to protect your rights.

Children and Childcare Arrangements

Legal issues involving children require sensitivity and clarity. Our team assists in matters such as residence, access, and financial support. We help parents and guardians reach fair and lasting agreements, always prioritising the best interests of the child. As trusted child arrangement order solicitors, we also use dispute resolution methods such as mediation where appropriate.

Cohabitation and Unmarried Family

Unmarried couples often face legal uncertainty when relationships end. Our family law firm offers advice on rights and entitlements, and we prepare cohabitation agreements to protect clients in the event of separation. We ensure financial responsibilities and property interests are clearly defined.

Dissolution of Civil Partnership

We provide practical and sensitive advice throughout the dissolution process, including financial matters and arrangements involving children. Our focus is on protecting your wellbeing and securing a fair outcome that reflects your needs and legal entitlements.

Family Mediation and Alternative Dispute Resolution

We support clients in resolving family disputes through mediation and other non-court alternatives. This approach often preserves important relationships and avoids the stress of litigation. Our family legal services promote cooperation and clarity during emotionally difficult times.

Financial Dispute on Divorce

Financial arrangements during divorce or separation can be complex. Our solicitors provide expert guidance on asset division, spousal maintenance, pensions, and high-net-worth cases involving international property or business interests. As experienced property dispute lawyers, we work with financial advisors to ensure outcomes are balanced and enforceable.

Injunction and Emergency Remedies

In situations involving domestic abuse or serious disputes, urgent legal action is often necessary. Our solicitors can assist with obtaining non-molestation orders and occupation orders to ensure immediate protection and stability. We also provide guidance on accessing additional community support where needed.

Trusted Guidance Throughout the Process

When you choose S A J Legal for family law matters, you receive personalised support every step of the way. From child arrangements and financial settlements to prenuptial agreements and litigation, we deliver practical legal solutions with empathy and professionalism. Whether you are seeking a child support lawyer or advice on protecting your property, our team is here to guide you toward a lasting resolution.

Pre-Nuptial and Post-Nuptial Agreements

While not legally binding, these agreements are increasingly recognised by UK courts. We draft tailored, fair agreements to protect our clients' assets and reduce the risk of future disputes. Legal advice is also provided for reviewing or signing such agreements with confidence.

Strong Representation in Court

Although we prioritise dispute resolution, some cases do require court action. If litigation becomes necessary, our solicitors work closely with top family law barristers in London to ensure your case is presented clearly and effectively. We will guide you through every step, with strategies that evolve with your case.
